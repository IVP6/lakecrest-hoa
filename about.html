About Lakecrest HOA

Meet The Board

Our dedicated Board of Directors is committed to maintaining Lakecrest's exceptional standards and fostering a vibrant community spirit. Comprised of passionate residents who volunteer their time and expertise, our board brings together diverse backgrounds in business, finance, property management, and community planning. They work tirelessly to ensure our neighborhood remains a premier place to call home, making thoughtful decisions that enhance property values, improve amenities, and create lasting memories for all families in our community.

Board Members

  • Brittany Cortinas is a devoted community leader and current President of the Lakecrest Forest and Village HOA Board. Since moving to the neighborhood with her family in October 2018, she has embraced every opportunity to serve and strengthen the community. Brittany is deeply committed to making Lakecrest a place where families feel safe, connected, and proud to live. She works tirelessly to enhance the beauty of the neighborhood, foster transparency, and create meaningful opportunities for residents to come together through fun, inclusive events. Her service is rooted in genuine care, and she considers it a privilege to give back to the community she and her family call home.

    Brittany Cortinas

  • Jessi Orsini is a dynamic force of resilience and creativity. Originally from outside Allentown, Pennsylvania, she joined the U.S. Army at eighteen, serving as a medic in the reserves for eight years—a role that honed her discipline, compassion, and leadership. While pursuing Marine Biology at Clearwater Christian College, she met her husband in St. Petersburg, Florida, and later moved to Houston, Texas, where they now raise five children. Jessi’s professional path spans hospitality, healthcare, and entrepreneurship. She owns a thriving soap-making business and is a published author, blending scientific curiosity with artistic flair. Whether mentoring others, building community, or exploring the outdoors with her family, Jessi leads with heart, grit, and a passion for growth.

    Jessi Orsini
